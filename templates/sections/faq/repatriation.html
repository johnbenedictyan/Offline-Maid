D. Repatriate (3)

When maid’s contract end, employer needs to buy a direct air ticket including check-in luggage to the international airport of the maid’s hometown or nearest to her hometown. If transit is required, employer should give sufficient allowance for maid to sustain herself during her transit.

The maid's departure date must be within 2 weeks after cancelling their work permit

All outstanding employment issues must be resolved before the departure date.

Employers are responsible for the cost of sending their Maid home even if the contract has not ended. This is to prevent a situation where the maid is left stranded in Singapore with no other means to return home.

Employers should book the Maid on another flight home and login to WPOL to extend her special pass and change her departure date.
